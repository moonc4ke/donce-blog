# Donce Blog

Donce Blog is Donatas Adomavicius's personal site and public portfolio. This
glossary keeps public-facing wording consistent across the site, profile copy,
and portfolio material.

## Language

**Featured Work**:
Public-safe summaries of current or recent work that may include private or
client-owned projects without exposing private access or operational details.
_Avoid_: Case study when the work cannot be fully disclosed

**Breezit Work**:
Public-safe portfolio language for work around Breezit backend services,
platforms, internal tools, and AI assisted engineering workflows.
_Avoid_: Private repo dump, customer data, production internals

**AI Assisted Development Workflow**:
A deliberate engineering workflow that uses tools such as Codex, Claude Code,
Slack bots, Autopilot loops, reviews, replay tools, and safety checks to build
or verify software.
_Avoid_: AI magic, vibe coding, generic AI tools

**Synthetic Replay Test**:
A controlled replay scenario created from source-controlled fixtures or safe
captured patterns to verify AI generated code and customer communication flows.
_Avoid_: Invented synthetic test, fake test

**Self-Hosted Stack**:
Personal infrastructure and tooling operated directly by Donce, including
Linux, Arch, Docker, Kamal, Caddy, Neovim, and home-server work.
_Avoid_: Homelab when the writing is for non-technical readers
